Hyapatia's first album was "The Two Sides of Hyapatia Lee" which featured comedy and country music. "Rub-A-Dub-Dub" was featured on the Dr. Demento show and made it to number three on the top Funny Five. Hyapatia began writing original music and formed a band in Los Angeles. The album Double Euphoric was released in 2000. Hyapatia opened for The Scorpions, Paul Rogers, and Ricky Medlock.
